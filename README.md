# TransitOps – Smart Transport Operations Platform

TransitOps is a centralized fleet management platform designed to digitize transport operations for logistics organizations. It replaces manual workflows with an integrated system for managing vehicles, drivers, trips, maintenance, fuel, expenses, and operational analytics. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Problem

Traditional fleet management often relies on spreadsheets and manual records, leading to scheduling conflicts, poor fleet utilization, missed maintenance, inaccurate expense tracking, and limited operational visibility. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Objective

Build an end-to-end transport operations platform that streamlines fleet management, enforces business rules, and provides real-time operational insights for efficient decision-making. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
